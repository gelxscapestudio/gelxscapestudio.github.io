Common Questions

What services do you offer at GelXscape Studio?
We specialise in gel nail extensions, gel polish, and clean dry manicures - designed for neat, elegant, and long-lasting results with a lightweight, natural finish.
How long will my nails last?
Most sets last 2-3 weeks before a refill is needed. With proper care, your nails stay glossy, strong, and low-maintenance throughout.
Will gel extensions damage my natural nails?
No - when done correctly. We follow a careful prep, application, and removal process to protect your natural nails at every step.
What makes GelXscape Studio different?
Each set is detail-led, lightweight, and designed to look natural - not bulky. The focus is on hygiene, structure, and a refined, wearable finish.
How do I book an appointment?
To confirm your appointment, message on Instagram or WhatsApp with your preferred service and date. A ₹100 non-refundable booking fee is required to secure your slot (adjusted in your final bill). Slots are limited and fill quickly, so early booking is advised. Rescheduling is allowed with at least 24 hours' notice; last-minute changes may result in the booking fee being forfeited.
How long does an appointment take?
Appointments typically take between 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on the service and design. This ensures proper prep, precision, and a clean, long-lasting finish without rushing.

Service Guide ✨

Understand our art and find your perfect look

Gel Polish

Like regular nail paint but way better. Applied on your natural nails after proper cleaning and prep, it stays chip-free and glossy for 2-3 weeks without peeling.

Gel Overlays

A thin builder gel coating applied over your natural nails to make them stronger and prevent breakage - no extra length added. Lasts 2-3 weeks with an infill option.

Temporary Extensions

Artificial length added to your nails, perfect for a wedding, function, or just trying extensions once. Designed for short-term wear of 1-2 weeks.

Semi-Permanent Extensions

Extensions that stay on for 4-6 weeks. As your nails grow, you simply come in for an infill to fill the gap - no need to remove and redo everything each time.

Permanent Extensions

For those who always want their nails done, all the time. You come in every 3-4 weeks for a refill as your nails grow - like a regular haircut, but for your nails.
