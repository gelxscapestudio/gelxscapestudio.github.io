Frequently Asked Questions 💅🏻

Everything you need to know about your next nail appointment at gelXscape studio.

1. What services do you offer at GelXscape Studio?
We specialise in gel nail extensions, gel polish, and clean dry manicures. Our focus is on lightweight, natural-looking nails with a refined finish rather than bulky extensions. We also offer minimal nail art like French tips, chrome, cat-eye, and subtle accents.
2. How long do gel extensions last?
With proper care, gel extensions typically last 2-3 weeks before a refill is needed. Longevity depends on your nail growth, lifestyle, and how well aftercare is followed.
3. Are gel extensions damaging to natural nails?
No - when applied and removed correctly, gel extensions do not damage your natural nails. We prioritise thorough prep, proper application, and safe removal techniques to maintain nail health.
4. How should I take care of my nails after the appointment?
Avoid using your nails as tools, keep them dry when possible, and apply cuticle oil regularly. Proper aftercare helps maintain shine, strength, and longevity of your set.
5. How long does an appointment take?
Depending on the service, appointments usually take between 1.5 to 3 hours. Detailed nail art may take longer. We focus on precision and quality, so we don't rush the process.
6. Do I need to book in advance?
Yes, appointments are strictly by booking. Since this is a home-based studio with limited slots, we recommend booking in advance atleast 2 days prior to secure your preferred time.
7. I've never tried gel extensions before - are they right for me?
Absolutely. If you prefer clean, polished, low-maintenance nails that don't chip easily, gel extensions are a great starting point. We keep the structure lightweight and natural, so it never feels overwhelming for first-time clients.
8. Do you customise nail shapes and designs?
Yes - every set is tailored to your nail shape, lifestyle, and preference. Whether you like short and minimal or slightly more detailed accents, the goal is always a balanced, wearable look that suits you.
9. What makes GelXscape Studio different from other nail studios?
The focus here is on detail, hygiene, and a refined finish. We don't rush appointments or create bulky sets - each nail is carefully structured to look natural, feel comfortable, and last well.
10. Can I get nail art along with extensions?
Yes, we offer minimal and elegant nail art like French tips, chrome, cat-eye, and subtle accents. Designs are kept clean and timeless to complement your overall look rather than overpower it.
11. What if I have weak or short natural nails?
That's completely fine. Gel extensions are designed to add strength and structure, making them ideal for clients with short or uneven nails. We build them in a way that still feels lightweight and natural.
12. Do you take last-minute appointments?
Occasionally, if a slot opens up - but most appointments are pre-booked. To avoid missing out, it's always better to schedule in advance.
13. How often should I come back for maintenance?
Refills are recommended every 2-3 weeks to keep your nails looking fresh and well-balanced. Regular maintenance also helps preserve the health of your natural nails.
14. Do you remove previous extensions done elsewhere?
Yes, we can safely remove previous work before starting your new set. Proper removal is important to maintain nail health, which is why we follow a careful, damage-free process.
15. Is this suitable for brides or special occasions?
Definitely. Many clients book for weddings, events, or vacations because of the long-lasting, polished finish. It's one less thing to worry about during important moments.
16. How early should I book my appointment?
We recommend booking at least a few days in advance, especially for weekends or special occasions. Limited slots ensure each client gets full attention, so early booking helps you secure your preferred time.
17. What hygiene practices do you follow?
Hygiene is taken seriously - tools are properly sanitised, and the process is clean and controlled. Since this is a home-based studio, the environment is calm, private, and maintained with strict hygiene standards.
18. Will the extensions feel heavy or uncomfortable?
Not at all. The extensions are designed to be lightweight and balanced, so they feel natural on your nails. Most clients forget they're even wearing extensions after a day or two.
19. What if I'm not sure what design to choose?
You don't need to come with a fixed idea. We can guide you based on your style, nail length, and occasion to create something that looks elegant and suits you perfectly.
20. Do you offer very long or dramatic nail styles?
Our focus is on neat, elegant, and wearable nails. While we can customise length to an extent, the signature style leans toward refined, natural-looking sets rather than extreme lengths.
21. Why are your services priced the way they are?
Pricing reflects the time, precision, quality products, and hygiene standards involved. Each appointment is detail-focused and unhurried, ensuring you receive a finish that lasts and looks premium.
22. Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
Yes, with prior notice of atleast 24 hours. Since slots are limited and reserved exclusively for you, timely updates help manage the schedule efficiently for all clients.
23. Do you work with working professionals and tight schedules?
Yes - appointments are structured and pre-planned to respect your time. Booking in advance helps us allocate a slot that fits smoothly into your schedule.
24. What should I do before coming for my appointment?
Arrive with clean nails and avoid cutting cuticles or removing products harshly at home. This allows us to prep your nails properly and achieve the best results.
25. Can I message you for queries before booking?
Of course. If you have doubts about services, designs, or suitability, feel free to reach out - we're happy to guide you so you can book with confidence.
